Health Education Resources

Healthy Eating

A balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins helps you stay energized and prevent diseases. Limit sugar, salt, and processed foods.

Exercise & Fitness

Regular physical activity improves cardiovascular health, strengthens muscles, and boosts mental well-being. Aim for 30 minutes a day, 5 days a week.

Mental Wellness

Take care of your mental health by managing stress, getting enough sleep, practicing mindfulness, and seeking help when needed.

Disease Prevention

Preventive healthcare includes regular check-ups, vaccinations, screenings, and maintaining good hygiene to avoid illness.

Hydration

Drinking enough water daily helps regulate body temperature, keep joints lubricated, and flush out toxins. Aim for 8–10 glasses a day.

Sleep & Recovery

Adults need 7–9 hours of sleep nightly. Quality sleep improves memory, immune function, and emotional stability.
